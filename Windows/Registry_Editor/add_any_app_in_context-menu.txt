1. Win+R [regedit]: to open "Registry Editor".

2. Go to "HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Directory\background\shell"
    
3. write-click at shell\New\Key\rename as <appName>

3. write-click at <appName>\New\String Value\rename as "Icon"

4. Win+S [notepad]

5. right-click on "Notepad" and go to "Open file location".

6. again right-click on "Notepad" and go to "Open file location".

7. Shift+right-click on "notepad.exe" then click at "Copy as path".

8. double-click at "Icon" and under "Value data:" paste path of your app using "Ctrl+V".

9. right-click on <Notepad>\New\Key\rename as "command"

10. In the right pane, double-click the (Default) value.

11. In "Value data:" box, paste the full path to the application's executable (.exe).

12. Open "Command Prompt" as "Run as Administrator" and fire the command below:
    taskkill /f /im explorer.exe & start explorer.exe
